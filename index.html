How to play UNO!

Let's play UNO!

Grab a friend and some cards and take a seat!!

What do you need?

  • One complete set of UNO cards
  • At least one friend!

Instructions

  1. Distribute the cards, seven to each player
  2. Place one of the remaining, undistributed cards in the middle, face up
  3. Pick a player to go first, this player must place a card of their own, which matches either the colour or number of the deteral card, atop that card
  4. Proceed in a clockwise fashion! +2/4 cards make the next player pick up that amount of cards from the extra pile of cards, reverse cards reverese the order of play (clockwise to counterclockwise), and wild cars allow you to pick the colour the next player must match!
  5. Have fun! Call out UNO!!! when you have 1 card left!
